Game Documentation

Complete guide to Neon Nexus City gameplay and features

Game Overview

Neon Nexus City is a futuristic cyberpunk mini GTA5-like game featuring aggressive action, thrilling combat, and dynamic gameplay. Explore the neon-lit city, complete missions, engage in combat, and drive vehicles in this immersive 3D experience.

Controls

Movement: WASD keys to move around the city
Sprint: Hold Shift to run faster (uses stamina)
Jump: Space bar to jump
Camera: Press C to toggle first-person/third-person view
Shoot: Left mouse click to fire weapon
Weapon Switch: Press 1, 2, or 3 to switch weapons
Vehicle: Press E near a vehicle to enter/exit
Drive: WASD to control vehicle, Space to brake
Menu: Press M to open/close game menu

Weapons

Pistol (Key 1): Balanced weapon with moderate damage and fire rate

Rifle (Key 2): High damage, slower fire rate, good for precision

Shotgun (Key 3): Massive damage, close range, slow reload

Game Systems

Health & Stamina

Monitor your health bar at the top of the screen. Stamina depletes when sprinting and regenerates when standing still.

Wanted Level

Cause chaos and your wanted level increases. Higher wanted levels mean more police attention and danger.

Threat Level

Shows how many enemies are actively targeting you. Stay aware of your surroundings!

Combo System

Kill enemies in succession to build a combo multiplier, increasing damage up to 3x.

Money System

Earn money by completing missions and defeating enemies. Use it to progress.

Missions

Elimination: Defeat a certain number of enemies

Delivery: Reach a marked location on the map

Survival: Survive waves of enemies for a set time

Tips & Tricks

  • Use vehicles to escape danger quickly
  • Build combos for increased damage
  • Manage your stamina for effective sprinting
  • Switch weapons based on enemy distance
  • Watch your threat level indicator
  • Complete missions for rewards
